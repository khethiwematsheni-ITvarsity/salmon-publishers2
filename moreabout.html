Author and Brands...

Mome Mahlangu – DEPTH Of BLACK LOVE.

A book that aims to mend broken families and provide resolutions for toxic in-laws. Mome reflects on her own journey to wellness as a makoti to husband Mongezi “Tall A$$ Mo”.

Talifhani “Mr Banks” Mamafha” & Xolani “Mr Kika” Gule – Beyond Ambition

The Blueprint, this book decodes and unpacks mysteries and secrets in personal and business development. Mr Banks writes on his defining moment, quitting a 45K monthly salary to start his own company in 2017, Analytics Advertising (AA)

Zanele “Dr Pru” Buthelezi – FORTITUDE

In this book, Dr Pru writes about her sister’s “Thabisile Buthelezi” unfortunate accident that crippled her at the age of 9. She further guides on ways to raise a disabled loved one as a family.

Harry Manche Phakoage – BORN TO LIVE

Principal Harry documents his journey from old South Africa to the new dawn. In this autobiography, he tells it all, his life in the village, managing being a pastor, a husband, a father and a teacher
